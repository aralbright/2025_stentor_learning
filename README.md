# Molecular changes during learning in the single cell, _Stentor coeruleus_

This repository accopmanies our 2025 study, "Molecular changes during learning in the single cell, _Stentor coeruleus_." _Stentor_ are giant single-celled protists capable of many complex behaviors, including regeneration and learning. Our study examines the changes in the transcriptome and proteome during learning and forgetting. Experiments were conducted by Deepa Rajan and analyzed by Ashley Albright. The file paths in all of these scripts reflect the local environment on Ashley's computer and would have to be changed if you choose to utilize this code.

## Experiment 1
Folder: 2025_stentor_learning/exp1

In this experiment, we examined 2 different training paradigms by RNA-sequencing: 4 hours of training or 19 hours of training.  

## Experiment 2
Folder 2025_stentor_learning/exp2

In this experiment, we examined 3 different training and forgetting paradigms by RNA-sequencing and proteomics: 19 hours of training, 19 hours of training + 10 minutes forgetting, and 19 hours training + 90 minutes forgetting.
